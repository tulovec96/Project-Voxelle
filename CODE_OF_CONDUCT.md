# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Project J.A.I.son is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community for everyone. We value the participation of every member and want to ensure that everyone has a positive experience.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- ✅ Being respectful and empathetic
- ✅ Welcoming different opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- ✅ Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- ✅ Focusing on what is best for the community
- ✅ Showing empathy towards community members
- ✅ Assuming good intent
- ✅ Helping newcomers feel welcome

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- ❌ Harassment, discrimination, or bullying
- ❌ Hate speech, slurs, or offensive language
- ❌ Personal attacks or insults
- ❌ Sexual harassment or unwelcome sexual attention
- ❌ Trolling, intentionally inflammatory comments
- ❌ Sharing others' private information without consent
- ❌ Spamming or self-promotion
- ❌ Any other conduct that violates someone's dignity or creates a hostile environment

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All community spaces (GitHub, Discord, forums, etc.)
- Public representations of the project
- Interactions with maintainers and contributors

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. **Discord**: Direct message a moderator
2. **Email**: conduct@jaison.dev (anonymous reports welcome)
3. **GitHub**: Report through [private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/limitcantcode/jaison-core/security/advisories)

All reports will be:
- ✅ Taken seriously
- ✅ Reviewed promptly
- ✅ Kept confidential
- ✅ Handled fairly

### Consequences

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may face:

1. **Warning** - First-time minor violations
2. **Temporary Mute** - For repeated or moderate violations (24-48 hours)
3. **Temporary Ban** - For serious or repeated violations (1-30 days)
4. **Permanent Ban** - For severe or persistent violations

Maintainers will use their best judgment in determining appropriate consequences.

---

## Appeal Process

If you believe a decision was unfair, you can appeal by:

1. Sending an email to conduct@jaison.dev within 7 days
2. Explaining why you believe the decision was unjust
3. Providing any additional context

Appeals will be reviewed by a different maintainer for impartiality.
